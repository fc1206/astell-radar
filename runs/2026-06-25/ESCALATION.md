# Escalation — 2026-06-25

## NEW TIER 1: Atolio (atolio.com)
- AI-powered enterprise search that indexes Slack, Google Drive, Confluence, Jira and other work apps with document-level permissions into a customer-controlled VPC/on-prem index, then answers questions across them; deployed privately (AWS/Azure/GCP/OpenShift).
- Why Tier 1: Cross-SaaS search + answers + source-permission mirroring is the whole product = Astell's exact lane; private/self-hosted deployment is the wedge.
- Evidence: https://www.atolio.com/

## NEW TIER 1: Squirro (squirro.com)
- Enterprise GenAI platform combining semantic enterprise search, knowledge graphs and conversational AI on a RAG architecture over a company's fragmented internal knowledge; markets explicit 'enterprise search' + 'knowledge management' offerings.
- Why Tier 1: Cross-knowledge search + answers + graph over org's own data = Tier-1 mechanics; heavy financial-services concentration and insights framing give it a data-intel lean, but the core promise matches Astell.
- Evidence: https://squirro.com/
